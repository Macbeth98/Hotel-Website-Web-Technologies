Interactive Kitchen Restuarant with Buffet

One of the finest buffet restaurants in Hyderabad located in Banjara Hills, The Dining Room is an all-day-dining restaurant with a theatrical open kitchen as its focal point. The restaurant offers guests, a comfortable and elegant setting with dishes cooked a la minute using premium meats and produce. The buffet spread for lunch and dinner offers the best of classics in Indian, Italian & Asian cuisines. In the a la carte, European dishes, authentic Indian cuisine and local Hyderabadi favourites like biryani and kebabs are the highlights.
